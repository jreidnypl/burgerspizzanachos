Island Burger

Burgers... patties, cheese, and buns. One of the best sandwiches ever invented. My favorite place is Island Burger. There are 3 locations along the west side. The small chain allows you to customize the burger with over 64 variations. They have an awesome happy hour special that include beer and fries for under $10!
