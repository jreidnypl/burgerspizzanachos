Blockheads

Whoever does not like guacamole, has no soul. Blockheads is a a great mexican restaurant with superb nachos that will leave your mouth in heaven. Particularly their nacho supreme will leave you full and happy. Plus you can't beat their $6.00 frozen margaritas. A nice afterwork spot that won't break your pockets.
